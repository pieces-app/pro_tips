## üë§ Personalization, Ownership, and Disambiguation

Pieces is getting noticeably better at understanding **what's yours, what belongs to others, and how your work fits together over time**. With our new **Personification and Disambiguation engines**, the app can build a more accurate picture of who you are, what you're focused on, and who you collaborate with‚Äîwithout blending contexts that shouldn't be mixed.

**Smarter Personal Context**

- Recognizes your writing style and technical preferences
- Builds a clearer picture of your expertise areas over time

**Better Collaboration Recognition**
- Accurately identifies who you work with most frequently
- Understands team dynamics and collaboration patterns
- Separates your contributions from team discussions
- Maintains context about who said what and when

**Contextual Intelligence**
- Summaries focus on what matters to *you* specifically
- Copilot responses grounded in *your* actual work
- Suggestions based on *your* patterns and preferences
- Memory formation aligned with *your* priorities
